About PEL

Powering lives with innovation, quality, and trust for generations.

Pak Elektron Limited (PEL) is one of Pakistan’s leading manufacturers of home appliances and electrical equipment, serving customers for over six decades. PEL was founded in 1956 through technical collaboration with AEG. In 1978, PEL was acquired by Saigol Group and was taken public a decade later. Over the years, PEL has formed alliances with several international giants, including General Electric, Fujitsu, and Hitachi.
